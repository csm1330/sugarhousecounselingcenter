About Tara

...and her passion for fat fueled living

Tara Finnerty

Registered Dietitian

Tara is a registered dietitian in Utah and owner of Sugar House Nutrition Clinic. Her aim is to provide nutrition support for people wanting to reap the many health benefits of the ketogenic (keto) diet. Her expertise in the keto diet was initially working with children who have uncontrolled epilepsy. The power of the diet fueled an appetite to study more about the benefits of nutritional ketosis in other areas of health and disease. The keto diet has been criticized as “unsustainable” and “impractical” however, when done with the right guidance to create a well-formulated, and personalized diet plan, the keto diet becomes a healthy lifestyle rather than just another diet. Tara supports nutrition diversity and works toward helping people find an individualized approach to make healthy eating sustainable.

Your journey begins today

The ketogenic diet is a low carb, moderate protein, and high fat diet which puts the body into a metabolic state known as ketosis.That's a pretty boring description but it is accurate. Essentially what this means is that when you figure out your macros (more on that later) then you'll find that you're eating mostly fats (these are good for you), some protein, and very little carbs.

Ingredients:
Chilli Ground/Mince Beef
500 g mince/ground beef
1 red onion quartered and sliced
2 cloves garlic crushed
1 tbsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground chilli
1 tbsp dried coriander
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp dried oregano
400 g tinned/canned chopped tomatoes or fresh

Instructions:Heat some oil in a saucepan and gently fry the onion and garlic until cooked but still soft. Add the mince/ground beef and continue to cook and stir until the meat is browned.Add the herbs, spices, and tomatoes. Stir thoroughly. Simmer and occasionally stir for 15 minutes while you prepare the crepes, vegetables and avocado salsa.
Recipe Notes:
The nutrition shows 1 serving of the chilli ground/mince beef. Serves 6.2 crepes would add Calories: 239 Fat: 19.4g Carbohydrates: 5.5g Fibre: 2.6g Protein: 10.2g The amount of avocado, sour cream and cheese has not been included as this will very incredibly between individuals.
